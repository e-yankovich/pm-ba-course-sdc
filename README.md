# CRM/ERP Implementation Project — BA & PM Case Study

> 📝 The project documentation is written in Russian.

A university course project covering Business Analysis and Project Management for a real estate agency's CRM/ERP system implementation. The case is built around a fictional company transitioning from scattered Excel-based workflows to a centralized system.

---

## BA Part

**Stakeholder analysis** using a Power/Interest Grid — categorizing founders, agents, legal, marketing, and support roles by influence and interest, with tailored requirements-gathering strategies per group (IDI interviews, workshops, shadowing, focus groups).

**Change management strategy** for resistant senior agents ("старички") who generate 55% of revenue — based on the ADKAR model with individual engagement tactics to avoid sabotage.

**Business requirements (BR-01 – BR-15)** covering: unified client database, lead management, sales pipeline, real estate object cards, document management, commission tracking, and analytics.

**Feature Catalogue** — 10 features grouped from the BRs, each linked to stakeholders and business problems.

**Epic backlog** — 9 epics prioritized by MoSCoW (Must Have / Should Have / Could Have), from core CRM and role-based access to analytics and buyer/tenant support.

**Use Cases** — 20 use cases with full specifications for key flows: creating clients, auto-capturing leads, distributing leads, managing deals, document lifecycle, and dashboard views.

**AS-IS process documentation** — description of the current fragmented state: Excel sheets, phone-based handoffs, no standard pipeline.

---

## PM Part

**Methodology selection** — hybrid approach: Scrum for product increments, Kanban for integrations and defects, Stage-Gates for milestone decisions with founders.

**Project team structure** — roles defined: Project Sponsor (Founder 1), Business Owner (Founder 2), Product Owner (Head of Sales), PM, BA, Solution Architect, Backend/Frontend developers, QA, DevOps, UX/UI Designer, Change Manager, Data Steward, SMEs from the business side.

**Risk register** — 10 risks identified and assessed (probability × impact), including agent resistance, low adoption, data quality issues, scope creep, integration failures, and legal/GDPR risks. Includes a Risk Heatmap.

**Rollout plan** in three phases:
- Phase 1 (MVP): client base, leads, sales pipeline, role model
- Phase 2: real estate objects, document management, basic analytics
- Phase 3: commissions, advanced analytics, buyer/tenant support

**User onboarding sequence**: new agents and power users first → marketing and sales management → full rollout including resistant senior agents and founders last.

**Testing strategy**: functional → integration → UAT → pilot before full launch.
